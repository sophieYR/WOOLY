recipe

  1. slice the onions into rings
  2. slice the tomato into thin slices
  3. cut the lettuce until you have individual leaves
  4. heat some olive oil in a pan at a high heat
  5. put the burgers onto the pan and turn them every few minutes until cooked
  6. toast the burger buns
  7. put ketchup, onions, lettuce and tomato on the toasted buns
  8. put the burgers on the bun
  9. eat
